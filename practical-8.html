Vision

To impart quality technical education through research, innovation, and teamwork to create professionally superior and ethically strong manpower. to foster an environment that empowers people, organizations, and societies through education, innovative ideas, impactful research, and transformative training. The university aims to cultivate a global reputation for excellence, achieve national and international rankings, and inspire innovative thinking and leadership in its students to address societal and planetary challenges. Mission

  • Provide ICT-enabled teaching and learning environment.
  • Enable students to become globally accepted engineers and professionals.
  • Provide knowledge and skills to succeed as engineers and entrepreneurs.
  • Prepare graduates for lifelong learning and ethical challenges.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  • PSO1: Demonstrate skills and competence in analysis, design, and development of computer-based systems addressing industrial and social issues.
  • PSO2: Tackle future technological challenges in areas like AI, IoT, Cyber Security, AR/VR, etc.
